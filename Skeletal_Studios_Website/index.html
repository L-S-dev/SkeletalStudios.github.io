Skeletal Studios

Skeletal Studios is an independent passion-driven development effort founded and run by L.S. It focuses on experimenting, learning, and building original software and games from the ground up.

What is Skeletal Studios?

Skeletal Studios is currently a solo project developed in free time, centered around programming-focused creativity. The work primarily involves creating a fully Java-based game engine and developing games built entirely on custom-written systems rather than pre-made frameworks.

The goal of Skeletal Studios is not rapid production or commercial output, but long-term growth, technical understanding, and the freedom to experiment with ideas that may not fit traditional studio pipelines.
